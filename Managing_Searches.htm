Managing Searches

Beyond ad hoc searches, you can save and share search queries in the Sumo Logic Web Application. To make it easier to manage all of your saved and shared search queries, they are saved in the search library. In addition, you can create scheduled searches that alert you to situations that could require immediate attention.
